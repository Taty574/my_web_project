Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to what we get asked most often about ordering, delivery, and events.

We currently deliver within Nairobi and immediate surrounding areas (Kiambu, Kajiado border towns) for an additional fee. For events further out, our event team can travel — get in touch via the Orders page with your venue location for a quote.

We recommend booking 6–8 weeks ahead for weddings so we can secure the right stems from our growers, especially for varieties that are only in season for a few weeks. Smaller arrangements and single bouquets can usually be arranged with 3–5 days' notice.

Most bouquets stay fresh for 5–7 days with a daily water change and stem trim. Hardier stems like chrysanthemums and eucalyptus can last up to two weeks. See our Diary for a full guide on extending vase life.
