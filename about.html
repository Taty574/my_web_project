Our Story

Where art meets nature. Meet our team of dedicated floral designers committed to bringing sustainable elegance and handcrafted beauty into your everyday spaces.

Founded with a vision to honor the raw, wild beauty of nature, The Floral Boutique has grown from a small studio workshop into a full-scale botanical design house. Our philosophy relies heavily on texture, movement, and a deep reverence for the seasons. We deliberately reject stiff, overly manicured arrangements in favor of organic shapes that let flowers behave exactly as they would in the wild.

Madeline Ross

Founder & Master Florist

With over a decade of experience styling botanical spaces across Europe and Africa, Madeline Ross views floral design as a form of living sculpture. Trained in classical European and African design techniques, she infuses every project with an editorial eye, prioritizing rare heirloom varieties and architectural branches that provide unexpected depth.
